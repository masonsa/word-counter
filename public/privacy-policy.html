Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Word Counter Tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our word counter tool.

2. Information We Collect

Our word counter tool is designed to be completely private and secure. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the text you input into the tool. All processing happens locally in your browser.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any text input, there is no information to use or store. The word counting process happens entirely on your device.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for the basic functionality of our website. We also use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site, but this does not track or store any of the text you input into the word counter.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics - for website analytics
  • Google AdSense - for displaying advertisements

These services have their own privacy policies, which you can review on their respective websites.

6. Data Security

Since all text processing happens locally in your browser, your data never leaves your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect our website and services.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@wordcounter.example.com
